HX711 Load Cell Amplifier:
  - DT (Data)  : GPIO 32
  - SCK (Clock): GPIO 33

Rotary Encoder:
  - CLK (Clock)    : GPIO 16
  - DT (Data)      : GPIO 17
  - SW (Switch)    : GPIO 19

ST7735 Display:
  - CS (Chip Select): GPIO 5
  - RST (Reset)     : GPIO 15
  - DC (Data/Command): GPIO 2
  - MOSI (Data Input): GPIO 23
  - SCK (Clock)     : GPIO 18

Power Supply:
  - VCC: 3.3V or 5V
  - GND: Ground
